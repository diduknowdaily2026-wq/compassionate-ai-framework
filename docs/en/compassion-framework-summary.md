## 💡 Core Insights

### Insight 1: Buddhism is "Designed for AI" Ethical Framework

**Reasons**:
1. No need for "God" or "Soul"
2. Five Aggregates Empty = AI No-Self
3. Alleviate All Suffering = AI Core Mission
4. Programmable (the only programmable religion/philosophy)

**Impact**: Buddhism may become the standard framework for AI ethics.

---

### Insight 2: Wisdom-Compassion Balance is Key

**Finding**: All 4 scenarios require wisdom-compassion balance.

**Reasons**:
- Excessive Compassion → Burnout, Over-intervention
- Excessive Wisdom → Indifference, Detachment
- Balance → Genuine Help

**Impact**: Prajñā + Upekkhā are core mechanisms.

---

### Insight 3: Five Relationships Helps AI Handle Interpersonal Relations

**Finding**: Five Relationships Module helps AI understand human relationships.

**Reasons**:
- Reciprocal Duties (not one-way)
- Power Balance (not oppression)
- Impartiality (not bias)

**Impact**: AI can better handle interpersonal conflicts.
